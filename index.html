⚠️ 重要注意事項Important Notes

行前必讀Must Read Before You Go

  • 確認燃油政策：通常 Full-to-Full，取車時確認油表並拍照Confirm fuel policy: usually Full-to-Full — check gauge and photograph at pickup
  • 取車時用信用卡預授權押金（通常 €500–1,000），取車後才扣Credit card hold for deposit (~€500–1,000) taken at pickup — not a charge
  • 帶台灣駕照 + 國際駕照（缺一不可！）Bring home driving licence + IDP — both required!
  • 確認保險：CDW 碰撞損失減免是否含括，建議補充超額保險Confirm CDW coverage — consider adding excess waiver
  • 取車時仔細拍攝車身 360° 照片，存入雲端備用Take 360° photos of car at pickup, save to cloud
  • 義大利有 ZTL 禁行區（限制通行區），進市區前確認，GPS 不一定提示Italy has ZTL restricted zones in town centres — GPS may not warn you!
  • 多洛米蒂山區收費停車場多，備妥 € 現金和硬幣Dolomites parking is often paid — bring € cash and coins
  • 三尖峰停車場只收現金！Tre Cime parking: cash only!
